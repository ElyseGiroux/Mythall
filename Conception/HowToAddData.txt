TO ADD A FEAT

	Fill D_FEATS_TABLE
	If required (Eg: Robustesse l), add the necessary data in R_FEAT_ABILITIES_TABLE
	Add the feat restrictions (R_FEAT_ALLOWED_RACES_TABLE, R_FEAT_ALLOWED_CLASSES_TABLE & R_FEAT_REQUIREMENTS_TABLE)


TO CREATE A CLASS:

		Fill D_CLASSES_TABLE
		Fill R_CLASS_ABILITIES_TABLE (Progression)
		Add the class restrictions (R_CLASS_ALLOWED_MULTICLASS_TABLE & R_CLASS_ALLOWED_ALIGNMENTS_TABLE)
		Add the required Abilities (Domains, Skills, Rogue Talents, Schools of Magic, etc.) and their requirements
